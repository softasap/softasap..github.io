    <h1>sa-queue-beanstalkd</h1>
    <p><a href="https://travis-ci.org/softasap/sa-queue-beanstalkd"><img alt="Build Status" src="https://travis-ci.org/softasap/sa-queue-beanstalkd.svg?branch=master" /></a></p>
    <p>beanstalkd_properties: list of regexes in form (regexp, line) to adjust /etc/default/beanstalkd with necessary changes</p>
    <p>Example of usage (all parameters are optional)</p>
    <p>Simple</p>
    <p>roles:
        - {
            role: "sa-queue-beanstalkd"
          }</p>
    <p>Advanced:</p>
    <p>roles:
        - {
            role: "sa-queue-beanstalkd",
            beanstalkd_properties:
              - {regexp: "^#START=.*", line: "START=yes"}
          }</p>
